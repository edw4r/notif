Privacy Policy for Notif

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Notif is an Android app that reads notifications aloud based on your settings.

1. Notification Listener Permission

Notif requires Android Notification Listener access (BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE) to read notification content and perform readout.

  • This permission is used only to process notification content for app features such as readout and related policy controls.
  • Notif does not use this permission to build user profiles or sell personal information.
  • You can revoke notification listener access at any time in Android system settings.

2. Information We Process

Notif processes notification content, app names, titles, message text, app-level preferences, and settings preferences on your device to provide core functionality.

3. How Processing Works

  • Notification content is processed on-device to support readout.
  • Notif is designed with privacy mode and does not persist notification-content history locally.
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.

4. Analytics and Firebase Data Collection

Notif uses Firebase Analytics to understand product usage and app behavior. Notif-defined analytics events may include app usage, feature interaction events, settings and policy interaction events, technical context, and app package name metadata related to policy interactions.

Notif does not send notification message text content to analytics events. Firebase may also automatically collect certain device/app identifiers and telemetry under Google's own documentation and policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Notif uses Google Firebase Analytics. These services process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Security Measures

  • Data sent to Firebase Analytics is encrypted in transit, for example via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Local app data is stored within Android app sandbox storage.

7. International Data Transfers

Firebase/Google services may process data on servers located outside your country of residence, including the United States and other jurisdictions.

8. Data Sharing and Sale

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not provide account-based data brokerage or user-profile sale.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Settings and app configuration data are stored locally on your device. You can clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored app settings/data.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information related to the app's processing activities. Because Notif is primarily local-device processing and does not require user accounts, our ability to identify and retrieve user-specific cloud records may be limited.

11. Children’s Privacy

Notif is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information in connection with this app, contact us and we will address the request.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this same URL with an updated effective date.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, contact edward.devapps@gmail.com.
